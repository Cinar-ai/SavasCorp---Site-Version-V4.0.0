Technology

Revolutionary systems powering the future of space travel

01

Raptor Engine

Our proprietary Raptor engine represents a breakthrough in rocket propulsion technology. Using full-flow staged combustion with liquid methane and liquid oxygen, it delivers unprecedented efficiency and power for deep space missions.

The engine's innovative design enables full reusability while maintaining exceptional performance across multiple launch cycles, dramatically reducing the cost of space access.

Sea-level thrust of 230 metric tons
Chamber pressure exceeding 300 bar
Fully reusable for 100+ flights
02

Autonomous Flight Control

Our AI-powered flight control system enables precise navigation and autonomous landing capabilities. Using advanced machine learning algorithms and real-time sensor fusion, our spacecraft can execute complex maneuvers with minimal human intervention.

The system processes thousands of data points per second, making split-second adjustments to ensure optimal flight paths and safe landings, even in challenging atmospheric conditions.

Real-time trajectory optimization
Precision landing within 10 meters
Adaptive thrust vectoring control
03

Advanced Materials

Our spacecraft utilize cutting-edge composite materials and thermal protection systems designed to withstand the extreme conditions of space travel. From cryogenic fuel tanks to heat shields capable of surviving atmospheric reentry, every component is engineered for maximum durability.

We employ proprietary carbon fiber composites, advanced alloys, and ceramic matrix materials that provide exceptional strength-to-weight ratios while maintaining structural integrity across temperature extremes ranging from -270°C to 1,500°C.

Lightweight carbon composite structures
Reusable heat shield technology
Cryogenic propellant management systems
